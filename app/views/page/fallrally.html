Frequently Asked Questions

What is FellCon?

So FellCon is an idea that started when we looked at the three pillars of APO. Leadership, Friendship, and Service. Sure the leadership and service elements of APO are super important and are the aspect of APO that many join for. However without the prospect of friendship and brotherhood, APO loses what makes it special. That's what we hope FellCon can do. We want to inspire and spread the tenants of fellowship and establish personal investment in or organization through an event focused on friendship.

Where is FellCon?

This year's FellCon will be hosted at Six Flags Magic Mountain in Valencia, California.

When is FellCon?

FellCon takes place Friday November 11th from 10AM till the parks closes. The actual conference section will take place from 11AM to 12PM.

Why should I go to FellCon?

Be part of the pioneering group to first participate in FellCon! It's going to be a day filled with fun and friendship, as we go to the basics of fellowship and simply just hanging out with our fellow brothers and sisters from all over. Not only that but profits from he ticket sales will go towards a charity! Details will be announced on which charity we will be supporting.

SOUNDS LIKE FUN! How do I sign up?

Go to our registration form and sign up through there. Make sure to venmo @RhoPi after!

Will housing be provided?

Sadly housing will be not be provided as Valencia is quite a ways away from San Diego. Although if you truly are in need of housing please contact us and we will see what we can do.
